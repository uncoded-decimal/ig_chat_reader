How to Download Your Instagram Data Archive

Go to your Instagram profile, open Settings, then tap "Your activity" and select "Download your information." Choose JSON format and request your data. Instagram will email you a download link, usually within 24-48 hours. Once downloaded, open the ZIP file here to read your chats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool safe to use?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files are never sent anywhere. The source code is open and inspectable.

What format does the Instagram export need to be in?

This viewer supports Instagram's HTML export format. When requesting your data from Instagram, make sure to select "HTML" as the format, not JSON.

Can I read old deleted messages?

You can read any messages included in your Instagram data export. Unless the users deleted their account. In that case it's better to sift through the archive yourself.

Does this work on mobile?

Yes, this Instagram chat viewer works on desktop and mobile browsers without needing to install any app.
